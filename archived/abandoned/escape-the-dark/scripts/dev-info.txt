===Story Setup===

You're a reacher sent to study this new "sculk" material, but a cave-in blocked your only exit.

Now, you must find a way to unblock it and escape, before the sculk overtakes you.


===Quest Shapes===

Circle: Default
Square: Technology
Rounded Square: Exploration
Hexagon: Challenges

Gear: Information
